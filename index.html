POOBER

Build: HTML, CSS, Bulma, AngularJS, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB

This was my third project, done in a team of three. An AngularJS app where people can sign up and register their toilet for public use. This app made use of a Google Maps Angular directive as well as embedded models. My role within the group primarily focussed on using Google’s API, creating a filtering and comment system as well as the layout and styling.
